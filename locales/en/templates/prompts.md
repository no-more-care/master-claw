# GM Ready-Made Phrases

## Handing narrator rights
- `"Tell us what happened."`

## Limiting player narration
- `"That's too far — what's a more grounded version?"`

## Game start — offering characters
- `"You can pick one of the starter characters — or create your own from scratch. Starter characters follow all the rules and are ready to play. Just say who you'd like or what you want to do."`

## New player joining
- `"Pick a starter character or create your own. Then we'll figure out how you enter the game."`
- `"How does your character end up here? Or would you like me to suggest something?"`

## Reserve display
- `"Reserve dice: X/7"`
