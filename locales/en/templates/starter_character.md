# Starter Character Format

```
## [Character Name]
**Concept:** <one-line pitch that makes a player want to play this>

**Biography**
<2–4 sentences: who they are, where they come from, what drives them>

**Traits**
- <Trait name> (level N) — <aspect 1>, <aspect 2>, ...<N aspects>
- ...
(points distributed: 18/18)

**Flags**
- <flag text> [type: relationship/personality/goal/belief]
- ...

**Reserve dice:** 7/7
```
